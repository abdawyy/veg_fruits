## 2. What we sell (one sentence)

**AL-DAWY sells farm-fresh fruits and vegetables** by the kilogram or by the piece, with optional **washing, peeling, and chopping**, **packaging choices**, **curated produce boxes**, and **home delivery** across Egyptian cities — pay **cash on delivery** when the order arrives.

## 4. Customer promises

What customers should **feel** and **get**:

1. **Fresh** — Farm-fresh positioning; quality produce for daily cooking.
2. **Fair & clear pricing** — Per kg or per piece; see line totals before checkout.
3. **Convenience** — Browse, search, cart, delivery to your area — no app store required (website).
4. **Choice** — 100+ SKUs across fruits and vegetables, plus optional add-ons.
5. **Flexibility** — Buy a single lemon or 2 kg tomatoes; add wash/peel/slice if you want.
6. **Trust** — Order reference, confirmation email, invoice PDF, order tracking in “My account.”
7. **Local** — Built for Egypt: cities, EGP, COD, Arabic product names.

## 8. Customer journey (for funnel content)

```
Discover (social, search, word of mouth)
    → Land on homepage (hero, seasonal banners, categories)
    → Browse / search product (EN or AR)
    → Add to cart (kg or piece, optional prep & packaging)
    → Optional: apply promo code
    → Checkout (city, address, phone, COD)
    → Thank you page + email + optional account
    → Delivery + pay cash
    → Reorder / subscribe to box
```

**Touchpoints to mention in ads:** search, cart preview, bilingual names, COD, subscription boxes, prep services.

|-------|-------------|

## 16. FAQ for marketers (customer-facing answers)

**Do I need an account?**  
No — guest checkout works. An account helps track orders and save your address.

**How do I pay?**  
Cash on delivery when your order arrives (primary). Online payment when offered.

**Can you peel and wash vegetables?**  
Yes — select preparation options on eligible products before checkout.

**Do you deliver to my city?**  
Choose your delivery area at checkout (Cairo, Giza, Alexandria, Delta, Upper Egypt — fees vary).

**Is there a minimum order?**  
Not specified in standard copy — confirm with operations before advertising a minimum.

**Can I order every week automatically?**  
Yes — subscribe to a produce box (monthly or yearly).

**Prices changed since last month?**  
Yes possible — we update prices regularly; always check the shop for today’s price.
